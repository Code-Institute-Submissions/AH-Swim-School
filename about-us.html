About us

We strongly believe that everyone of all ages should learn how to swim, not just for fun but also as an important life skill.

We are a family run swim shool business with a love for the sport. We have dedicated over 20 years to the sport between us, and this has gave us the experience needed to improve your ability as a swimmer.

So take a leap forward and have the trust and faith in us on your swimming journey!

What We Offer:

  • Swimming lessons for all ages
  • Fun and enthusiastic teachers
  • Small grouped lessons in order for you to get the maxium experience
  • Fully qualified level 2 and 3 swimming instructors
  • Further training methods for our older and advanced swimmers with a tailored gym programme
  • Award schemes and goals for our swimmers to see and show progression
  • Easy online booking
